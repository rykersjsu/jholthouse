The Queer Word, Netart, 2018

jholthouse.github.io/netart

For this piece I wanted to explore the idea of sharing information regarding queer culture. I have provided words from queer culture (plus their definitions), coming out stories, pictures of hate crimes, and significant people who have moved the queer community forward. I believe that information, communication, and sharing of feelings are the most important part of acceptance and this piece is the embodiment of that idea.

The Choices I've Faced, Netart, 2018

jholthouse.github.io/final_project

Everyone faces different things in life. However, as different as they are there is always a common thread that each subculture has in common with each other. In the LGBTQ community, just like every other community, we share a common thread in that we all face some of the same situations. In this piece I am opening up a part of my life for inspection. The names and faces may be different, but the situations, feelings, and choices are very real.
